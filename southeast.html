Top 10 Beaches — Southeast

This beaches include golden sands, craggy coasts, and beautiful blue waters, making them traveler favorites for anything from relaxing beach days to undersea activities.

1. Baia do Sancho

Fernando de Noronha, Brazil

Baia do Sancho is a small but very picturesque beach in Fernando de Noronha's eponymous secluded bay, which was designated as the first Brazilian marine park. This beach is invariably one of the top ten places in Brazil for a beach vacation year after year.
