About Us

Similar to you, we didn't know who to rely on for beach advise. We created BeautyOfBeaches, a beach website written by actual experts rather than anonymous reviewers, for this reason. Our writers include locals who are proud of their hometowns, parents who are road trip heroes, cruise enthusiasts who are familiar with every ship at sea, and essentially everyone else in between. Our team and an army of freelance travel editors vet and update each article on the site. Furthermore, We don't just recommend everything, but only the finest. Unlike other review sites, our writers use their local knowledge to recommend locations worth your time, whether you're on a work trip or a family holiday.

Ethics

BeautyOfBeaches editors and select contributors, like many other beach magazines, frequently collaborate with tourism boards, tour operators, and companies to supplement our coverage. In an era when many independent media sources are fighting to exist, this is one way to help our reporters on the road and give them personal access to areas and experiences that would otherwise be too expensive. These experiences enable TripSavvy to create rich, well-researched tales that provide a comprehensive perspective of a destination.
