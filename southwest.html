Top 10 Beaches — Southwest

This year's winning beaches have golden sands, rugged coastlines, and clear blue waters, making them traveler favorites for everything from lazy beach days to underwater adventures.

1. Cable Beach

Broome, Australia

"Great to sight see, walk, swim, rock pool and watch the sunset. We did all of this while having a great time."

place to stay:

  • 12 Hotels
  • 17 B&B and Inns
  • 18 Vacation Rentals

2. Manly Beach

Sydney, Australia

"A long, wide, sandy beach right on the Pacific Ocean's edge. There are walking and cycling paths, as well as some green lawn areas and a sandy beach."

place to stay:

  • 207 Hotels
  • 81 B&B and Inns
  • 508 Vacation Rentals

3. Emily Bay

Norfolk Island, Australia

The beach is well-known for its immaculate, hard-packed sand. “Pristine beach that never seems to end. Photographer's dream. A place in the sun that warrants returning again and again.”

place to stay:

  • 5 Hotels
  • 1 B&B and Inns
  • 18 Vacation Rentals

4. Mooloolaba Beach

Mooloolaba, Australia

"The sand, the wind, the water, the people, the view... I adore them all. A perfect beach vacation with friends."
place to stay

  • 16 Hotels
  • 4 B&B and Inns
  • 87 Vacation Rentals

5. Matira Beach

Bora Bora, Society Islands

"It's miles of powdery, white sand lined with palm trees. The turquoise waters are clear enough that you can see the fish swimming beside you."
place to stay

  • 16 Hotels
  • 10 B&B and Inns
  • 40 Vacation Rentals

6. Four Mile Beach

Port Douglas, Australia

"A lovely stretch of beach with excellent walking opportunities. Palm trees, white sand, and shallow waters line the beach."

place to stay:

  • 39 Hotels
  • 4 B&B and Inns
  • 486 Vacation Rentals

7. Whitehaven Beach

Whitsunday Island, Whitsunday Islands

"I've been around the world and honestly this is one of the most spectacular places on the planet. It is truly breathtaking, from the crystal clear turquoise water to the miles of pure white sand."

8. Tahunanui Beach

Nelson, New Zealand

"South Island's best beach."

place to stay:

  • 42 Hotels
  • 53 B&B and Inns
  • 172 Vacation Rentals

9. Turquoise Bay

Exmouth, Australia

"Words cannot express how beautiful this beach is. Long stretches of white sand and turquoise water. World-class snorkeling for both beginners and experts."

place to stay:

  • 5 Hotels
  • 1 B&B and Inns
  • 122 Vacation Rentals

10. Natadola Beach

Sigatoka, Fiji

“Amazing combination of white, velvet-like sands and water changing colors throughout the day, from light blue to emerald green.”

place to stay:

  • 12 Hotels
  • 10 B&B and Inns
  • 22 Vacation Rentals
